# Steps component demo

A numbered procedure with the connecting rail. Title and body stay visible;
only `StepDetail` collapses.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Register">
    Go to **StaffXP → Register** and choose today's class from the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark each learner">
    Tap a learner to cycle present, late, or absent. Changes save automatically
    as you go.

    <StepDetail summary="Keyboard shortcuts (advanced)">
      Press `P`, `L`, or `A` with a learner selected to mark them without the
      mouse. Use the arrow keys to move between learners.
    </StepDetail>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the summary" final>
    A summary appears at the top once every learner is accounted for. Check the
    totals, then you're done.
  </Step>
</Steps>
