Marking attendance

The register is where you record attendance for each class. It is one of the most used tools in StaffXP, designed so you can mark a full class quickly and dependably, then move on.

Open StaffXP → Register, choose the class, and mark each learner present, late, or absent. Changes save automatically as you go, and a summary appears at the top once every learner is accounted for.
